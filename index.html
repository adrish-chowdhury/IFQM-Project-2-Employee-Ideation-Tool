#IDA-2024-051
Automated safety checklist before shift start using QR scan
✕

Attachments
Yashas R (EMP-2021-0341) · QA Dept · Mysuru Plant
Rahul Mehta (EMP-2022-0187) · Safety Dept
Currently, safety checklists before each shift are done manually on paper forms. This leads to incomplete entries, delays in shift start, and difficulty in tracking compliance. Missing entries are only noticed during audits, by which time it is too late to take corrective action.
Implement a QR-code based digital safety checklist accessible via any smartphone. Each workstation gets a QR sticker. Workers scan before shift start; form auto-submits with timestamp. Non-compliance alerts supervisor instantly. Data stored centrally for real-time compliance tracking.
₹45,000 / year
